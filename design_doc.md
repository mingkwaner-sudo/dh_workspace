## Working hypotheses
The enduring "barren rock never be a mart for Trade" myth by Lord Palmerston (1840) is a colonial construct to legitimize British acquisition by downplaying Hong Kong's pre-1841 history. 
This project argues that Pre-colonial Hong Kong was not an empty wasteland but a strategically vital and economically integrated node within the Pearl River Delta's maritime network.
